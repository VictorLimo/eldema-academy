Why Do Volunteers Pay? Transparency & Impact

Your Fee Funds Your Stay

It covers your accommodation, two daily meals, airport pick-up, orientation, and 24/7 in-country support. This ensures a safe and smooth experience.

Direct Community Support

A guaranteed portion of every volunteer's fee goes toward supporting ongoing projects established Eldema Letap Academy.

Administrative Reliability

The $220 USD Registration Fee covers administration, pre-trip support, program promotion, and inspection costs for new & existing programs.

Essential Requirements

  • Age: Must be at least 18 years old. (Exceptions for supervised groups are possible).
  • Language: Must speak at least Intermediate English.
  • Attitude: Passionate, open-minded, and willing to embrace a new culture.
  • Medical Placements: Require specific medical training or qualifications.
